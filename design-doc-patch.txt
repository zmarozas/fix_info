### How we match updates back to the right subscription

`ProductId` on the BiFrost side is a **client-defined free-form string** -- BiFrost doesn't interpret it, just echoes it back. The bridge encodes the RIC (or whatever identifier the snapshot needs to route on) into `ProductId` when building the `PricingRequest`. On ongoing updates, `ProductId` comes back on the topic (`Pricing/GREEKS/{UnderlyingName}/{ProductId}`) and the bridge reads it from there to publish the snapshot to the right Blackbird destination.

This makes the bridge **fully stateless** -- no in-memory registry or correlation map. Confirmed with Mark; matches the BiFrost design intent.

*Small thing to verify during build:* whether `ProductId` is also present in the protobuf message body, or only on the Solace topic. JCSMP exposes the topic on every received message either way, so it's not a blocker -- just a one-line implementation detail.
